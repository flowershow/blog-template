Hi, I’m **Oliver James** — botanist, writer, and lifelong plant enthusiast.

I hold a Master’s degree in Botany from the University of Edinburgh and have spent the past decade working in plant ecology, native species conservation, and urban greening projects. My work blends scientific curiosity with hands-on practice, from field identification to indoor plant care and experimental cross-pollination.

_Rooted & Wild_ is where I share what I learn — and unlearn — through a life spent noticing plants.
