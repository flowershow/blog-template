I’m a botanist with muddy boots and soil-stained hands, equally at home hiking through wild meadows as I am tending to a row of sun-seeking houseplants on my windowsill. This blog is my way of documenting the quiet joy of paying attention to the natural world — one leaf, sprout, and flower at a time.

Here, you’ll find:

*Plant Spotting Adventures* — field notes and stories from my walks, with tips on identifying native and not-so-native flora in parks, forests, and city cracks.

*Houseplant Care* — how I keep my leafy roommates happy, thriving, and occasionally dramatic.

*Botanical Experiments* — a peek into the delicate world of cross-pollination, seed saving, and amateur plant breeding.

Whether you're a seasoned plant lover or just beginning to learn the names of your neighborhood trees, I hope this space encourages you to slow down, step outside, and rediscover wonder in the green things growing all around us.
